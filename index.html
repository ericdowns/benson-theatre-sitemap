Header & Footer

How people get around. Seven items is too many across the top, so the supporting pages tuck into three dropdowns (About, Rent the Theatre, Support), with Events and Education as direct links and Donate as the one highlighted button. The footer then carries a full index, so every supporting page is reachable from a dropdown, the footer, or both. The only two pages kept out of the navigation on purpose are Payments (reached by direct link only) and Green Room Campaign (archived).
